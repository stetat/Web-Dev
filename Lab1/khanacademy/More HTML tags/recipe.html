Recipe #1 - Pizza

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Dough 300g
Cheese 100g
Tomato a half

Step 1: Form a circle from dough
Step 2: Slice cheese and tomato, put them on top of the circle dough Step 3: Put it into oven, wait for 30 minutes, and you have your pizza

Source: BellyFull website


Recipe #2 - Steak

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Raw meat stake 750g
Salt 10g
Garlic 1 piece

Step 1: Sprinkle the salt over the steak
Step 2: Slice garlic into very small pieces, spread it over the steak Step 3: Put it into oven, wait for 1 hour, and you have your steak ready

Source: Panlasangpinoy website


Recipe #3 - Chicken soup

  • Time: 40 hour
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Raw chicken file 500g
Salt 30g
Water 3000ml

Step 1: Pour the water into the pot
Step 2: Slice chicken into small pieces, put it into the pot, and season it with salt
Step 3: Put it onto oven, wait for 45 minutes, and you have your chicken soup ready

Source: Downshiftology website
